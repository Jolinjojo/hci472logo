Logo Design Ideas

I have designed these logos for a family business. "M" and "J" are the first initials for the two business owners. I want clean, polished, and modern looks for all the logos while demonstrating unity, harmony, and love between them. The circle is a universal symbol of wholeness, totality, eternity, and perfection, so I create "M" and "J" in a circle, and use a ring to hold them both, to give the logos the sense of completion, confident and harmony. For colors, I choose timeless black and white, and ancient dark green and deep red. Black and white logos can be used on any color background. Dark green and deep red are the colors I choose from the traditional Asian colors matching theme to honor the family history and roots.
